Identity

Whenever people ask me what I believe in, who I am, or try to attribute any specific trait to me, I always shrug it off and say I’m just 'Seyitan Edwards' instead of agreeing or disagreeing unless it is something I'm sure I'm not. I do this so I can always keep an open mind and not lock myself into one thing. I think this is also why I struggle to pick a favorite of anything and even find it hard to answer hypothetical 'this or that' questions—because I never want to be associated with just one thing. I want to be a blank canvas or a full canvas of infinite colors, with space for new ones.
